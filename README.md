# 🌟 DHKHONI Executive Sales Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Power BI** for **DHKHONI Perfumes**, a perfume retail business operating across Saudi Arabia.

The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into:

* Sales performance
* Revenue trends
* Profitability
* Customer satisfaction
* Product category analysis
* Regional business performance
* Operational expenses

This project transforms raw business data into strategic insights that support smarter and faster decision-making.

---

# 🚀 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to help management:

* Monitor overall business performance
* Identify profitable regions and products
* Improve operational efficiency
* Analyze customer behavior
* Track KPIs in real time
* Support data-driven business decisions

# 📈 Business Insights

## ✅ Revenue Analysis

* Total revenue exceeded **SAR 78 Million**
* Jeddah and Taif generated the highest revenue
* Riyadh recorded the lowest sales performance

---

## ✅ Profitability Analysis

* Profit margin achieved **32.21%**
* Profit trends declined in recent years
* Operational and logistics expenses impacted profitability

---

## ✅ Product Category Analysis

Top-performing product categories:

1. Unisex Perfumes
2. Men Perfumes
3. French Perfumes
4. Women Perfumes
5. Oriental Perfumes

---

## ✅ Brand Performance

Highest contributing brands:

* Tom Ford
* Dior
* YSL
* Lattafa

Luxury perfume brands generated the strongest profitability contribution.

---

## ✅ Operational Cost Analysis

Warehouse and Delivery departments represented the highest operational salary expenses, indicating opportunities for logistics optimization and cost reduction.
